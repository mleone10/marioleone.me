About Me

Mario Leone · July 21, 2020

Hello!

I’m Mario! I am a software engineer specializing in cloud-native architecture, CI/CD optimization, and clean code practices. I currently work at Capital One in McLean, VA as a backend engineer on our consumer credit monitoring tool, CreditWise. On the side, I’m slightly obsessed with the Go programming language and serverless design.

When I’m not at work, I pursue a wide array of interests, including community theater (mostly with The Arlington Players), and D&D. I also enjoy running, journaling, reading, and discovering all that Northern Virginia has to offer.

Find me on GitHub, LinkedIn, or Twitter.
